About Us

At CC & Co, we are a dynamic team of seasoned professionals committed to delivering cutting-edge accounting, taxation, and advisory solutions. Established in October 2019 in Hyderabad, our consulting firm is built on a foundation of innovation and expertise, combining traditional practices with modern technologies to drive business excellence.

We specialize in direct tax, indirect tax, internal audits, statutory audits, and special engagements, all with a core focus on achieving better compliance management and fostering good corporate governance. Our team includes Chartered Accountants, Cost and Management Accountants, Company Secretaries, and we are closely associated with a consulting actuary to provide comprehensive services tailored to your business needs.

What sets us apart is our ability to integrate artificial intelligence and data analytics into our service offerings. By leveraging advanced tools and methodologies, we offer strategic insights that help businesses optimize processes, ensure compliance, and make informed decisions in an increasingly complex regulatory environment. Our forward-thinking approach allows us to not only navigate challenges but to transform them into opportunities for growth.

At CC & Co, we are dedicated to being more than just consultants – we are your partners in achieving financial success, ensuring that your business remains agile, compliant, and future-ready.

Our Services

ROC Services

  • Streamlining Regulatory Compliance with ROC Services:
    In the intricate world of corporate governance, regulatory compliance is essential for business credibility and success. At CC & Co, we offer comprehensive ROC services that ensure businesses adhere to the Registrar of Companies (ROC) regulations. Our client-focused approach simplifies compliance, allowing businesses to navigate the legal landscape with confidence.
  • Incorporation and Company Formation:
    We assist entrepreneurs in forming new companies, guiding them through incorporation, selecting the optimal business structure, and obtaining necessary approvals for seamless registration.
  • Annual Compliance Filings:
    We manage annual filings with the ROC, including financial statements, returns, and other documents to ensure timely and accurate compliance.
  • Changes in Corporate Structure:
    For businesses undergoing structural changes, we handle ROC-related documentation, from name and office changes to share capital adjustments.
  • Statutory Audits and Certifications:
    Our audits provide independent assessments of financial records, ensuring compliance with regulatory standards, accompanied by required certifications.
  • Board Meetings and Minutes Compliance:
    We help businesses maintain proper documentation of board meetings, ensuring adherence to ROC protocols and procedures.
  • Legal Due Diligence:
    For mergers, acquisitions, or partnerships, we conduct thorough due diligence, ensuring legal and regulatory compliance with ROC requirements.
  • Regulatory Liaison:
    We act as a bridge between businesses and regulatory authorities, streamlining approvals, addressing queries, and resolving regulatory issues.
